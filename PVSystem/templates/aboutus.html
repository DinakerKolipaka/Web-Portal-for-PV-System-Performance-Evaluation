About Us

The objective of the Solar PV System Performance Evaluation project is to create a common platform that would enable PV System Owners, Investors, Certification Bodies and other solar industry-related users to access and upload information about photovoltaic systems. The features of the application have been developed based on the requirements primarily gathered from industry standards documents, interviews and research papers. By providing users with useful and efficient features to exchange and access data about PV systems, the application aims to simplify the tedious processes like performance evaluation and certification of PV Systems.
